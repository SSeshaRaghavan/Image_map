KG Cinemas
KG Cinemas in Coimbatore is a historic, four-screen multiplex established in 1981, offering a long-standing, popular movie-watching experience. Known for its large seating capacity, the venue was recently renovated and rebranded as KG Multiverse in 2025, featuring upgraded,, plush seating, advanced projection, and Dolby Atmos sound for an immersive experience.
